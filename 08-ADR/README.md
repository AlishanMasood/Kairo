# Architecture Decision Records

## Purpose

This folder contains Architecture Decision Records (ADRs) that document significant technical decisions made during the development of the Kairo platform.

## What Belongs Here

- Architecture Decision Records following a consistent template
- Decision context and rationale
- Alternatives considered
- Consequences of decisions made
- Status tracking of decisions (proposed, accepted, deprecated, superseded)

## What Does NOT Belong Here

- General architecture documentation (use `04-Architecture`)
- Research or exploration notes (use `09-Research`)
- Product decisions without technical implications (use `02-Products`)
- Governance decisions (use `00-Governance`)
