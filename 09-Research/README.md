# Research

## Purpose

This folder contains research notes, explorations, spikes, and investigative documentation. Content here may be informal or incomplete and serves as a reference for future decisions.

## What Belongs Here

- Technology evaluations
- Proof-of-concept summaries
- Spike results
- Competitive analysis
- Literature reviews
- Feasibility studies
- Experimental findings

## What Does NOT Belong Here

- Finalized architecture decisions (use `08-ADR`)
- Approved product specifications (use `02-Products`)
- Official architecture documentation (use `04-Architecture`)
- Development guidelines (use `05-Development`)
